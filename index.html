Hello, I'm Yun Bai

About Me

I work at the intersection of materials science, mechanical engineering, and smart systems. My research explores programmable metamaterials, soft electronics, and adaptive structural systems for real-time mechanical functionality.

Research

  • Nature (2022): A self-evolving reprogrammable surface for shape morphing.
  • Nature Communications (2022): Fast electromagnetic actuation of liquid metal networks.
  • Digital solid-liquid composite: Manuscript in preparation on programmable 3D mechanical properties.
